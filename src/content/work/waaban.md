Waaban is an AI-powered market-analysis platform built to help people make sense of market information with more context and less noise.

I lead the company and the product direction, shaping the research, workflows, and user experience around the question of how AI can support better judgment instead of replacing it.

The problem is not a lack of market data. The problem is that people are often asked to interpret too much, too quickly, without enough structure. Waaban is my work on making that process clearer, more useful, and more humane.
