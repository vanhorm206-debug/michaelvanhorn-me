Waaban is an AI-powered market-analysis platform I co-founded and lead. The idea is simple: people are not short on market data, they are short on context. Waaban uses AI to add that context, so the goal is better judgment, not a machine making the call for you.

I lead the company on a three-person founding team, owning strategic direction, operations, and brand. That puts me across the whole product: the research behind it, the workflows users move through, and how it reads to someone who is not a specialist.

It is live and free to start. The hard problem is not the technology; it is making something genuinely useful and clear for real people, and keeping it that way as it grows.
