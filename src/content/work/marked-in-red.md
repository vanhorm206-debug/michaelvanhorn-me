Marked in Red is an interactive awareness map for Missing and Murdered Indigenous Women. I scoped, designed, and shipped it end to end.

It is built with Next.js, Supabase, and Leaflet: an interactive map layer over a structured data backend. The work was about turning something heavy and often abstract into something people can see and sit with.

This project is personal. I am Anishinaabe, a descendant of the Red Lake Nation, and MMIW is not a distant issue where I come from. Building it was a way to put the same care I bring to people-centered work toward a community the data too often overlooks.
